About Us

Admission Process

Welcome to Shikha Public School! We are excited to have you join our school community. Our admission process is designed to ensure a smooth and transparent experience for both parents and students.

To apply for admission, please follow these simple steps:

  1. Fill out the online admission form available on our website.
  2. Submit the required documents, including birth certificate, previous academic records, and any other relevant certificates.
  3. Attend the admission interview with our faculty to assess the student's suitability for our academic programs.
  4. Upon successful completion of the interview, you will receive an admission offer.
  5. Complete the enrollment process by submitting the required fees and completing any additional paperwork.

If you have any questions or need assistance during the admission process, feel free to contact our admission office. We look forward to welcoming you to Shikha Public School!
